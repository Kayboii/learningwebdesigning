Introduction To Web Designing

First of all , what is Web Designing. Many of us in the compter field hear about WebDesigning freequently and begin th wonder, "what is Web Designing?"

Web Designing is the process of assembling codes into a text editor like vscode, brackets, sublinetext,etc in a specific mark up language which is "HTML"(hyper text mark-up language)and most often using another Web Designing language called "CSS"(cascading style sheet) and "JS"(javascript). Note that Web Designing can be more effective and easy while using a computer or a laptop.

How To Learn Web Designing

there are three basic ways of learning Web Designing of which are very porpular. They include

  1. Taking online courses
  2. Learning from a teacher or mentor
  3. Downloading Web Designing videos

Amongs this three methods of learning Web Designing, the most effective method is the method of learning form teachers or mentors. This is very effective beacuse you as a learner in Web Designing have opportunity to ask you teacher or mentors question and express your feeling to him or her. The method of downloading Web Designing videos are not very effective because the person doing the Web Designing in the video may not be able to explain some html tags to you and you may not know the use of certain html tags. The method of taking online courses are also effective but are not as effective as learning from teachers and mentors because taking online courses needs a lot of resources, example are Internet (not most people are able to he acsess to internet or have such resources.)
